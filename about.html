About Our Academy

Our Story

Founded in 2015, NURUL YAQIN ONLINE QURAN ACADEMY began with a simple mission: to make authentic Quranic education accessible to Muslims worldwide through online learning.

Our Vision

To become a leading global platform for Quranic education that preserves traditional Islamic teaching methods while leveraging modern technology.

Our Methodology

  • One-on-one sessions with qualified teachers
  • Structured curriculum based on classical Islamic sciences
  • Interactive learning through video conferencing
  • Regular progress assessments
